The Three Lions in Atlanta to boost a shaky start: at 18:00, all out to overcome the Congo athletes

By: Champion's Report Journal Published: July 1, 2026 Updated: 15:00 CEST

The Background and Latest News

The English national team reaches this crucial crossroad after managing to advance from the group stage with more friction than expected in their buildup play. Tuchel has to deal with major injuries in the backline, but can count on maximum firepower in the final third. On the other hand, the squad managed by Sébastien Desabre arrives at this historical fixture fueled by an impressive collective compactness, determined to exploit every quick transition to pull off what would be the most shocking upset of this World Cup edition.

Key Tactics and Match Elements

The British strategy will center heavily around rhythm management and the creative flair of Jude Bellingham, supported on the wings by the pace of Bukayo Saka and Marcus Rashford. In midfield, the shield formed by Declan Rice and the surprising Elliot Anderson will have to contain the quick African vertical transitions. In fact, DR Congo will shape up with a defensive but flexible five-man backline, ready to trigger the explosiveness of Yoane Wissa to surprise an unedited and heavily rotated English defense due to recent injuries.

The Key Battle: Mbemba vs Harry Kane

The outcome will inevitably be decided inside the final sixteen meters. The solid Congolese captain, Chancel Mbemba, will lead the defensive unit with the specific task of shutting down Harry Kane. The experienced Bayern Munich striker, Tuchel's primary offensive weapon, will look to link up play for late runs and exploit any tiny hesitation in the African defense. This direct clash of extreme physical intensity will be the true turning point to see who faces Mexico in the next round.
